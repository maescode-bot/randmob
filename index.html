HOW DOES MOBKEY AND MOBCODE WORK:

THESE ARE UNIQUE IDENTIFIERS, USED TO ENHANCE EFFICIENCY AND SECURITY OF OUR PLATFORM. EVERY USER HAS A MOBCODE WHILE EVERY ORGANIZATION IS ASSIGNED A MOBKEY. HOW SAFE ARE THE CAPTURED DATA?

OUR PLATFORM IS BUILT WITH THE NECESSARY SECURITY CERTIFICATES. USERS DATA ARE ALSO NOT SHARED WITH THIRD PARTIES, EXCEPT WITH RELEVANT HEALTH AUTHORITIES.

WHY MECHANICS IN A HEALTHCARE PLATFORM?

ASSETS, ACCEPTED AS SECURITY ARE AUTOMOBILES, THAT NEED REPAIRS AND MAINTENANCE. WE PROVIDE THESE SERVICS TO ENSURE THAT THESE ASSETS ARE IN TOP SHAPE, TO REDUCE TIME IT TAKES TO ACCESS OUR CREDIT SERVICES.
ALL MECHANICS ARE RECOMMENDED BY USERS FROM PAST RELATIONSHIPS AND VERIFIED BY OUR TEAM. THE METHOD OF SERVICE DELIVERY IS BETWEEN THE USERS AND THEIR CHOSEN MECHANICS PER REPAIR CARRIED OUT. THE WARRANTY PERIOD NEGOTIATED WILL BE ENFORCED BY RANDMOB.

RANDMOB AS A CREDIT PLATFORM, POWERS SOFTWARE AND SERVICES FOR THE HEALTHCARE INDUSTRY. WE BRIDGE THE GAP BETWEEN PATIENTS AND CARE GIVERS, USING FINANCE AND TECHNOLOGY.
